    Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

    1. What are the best streams after 10th?
    The best stream depends on your interests and career goals. Science for engineering/medical, Commerce for business/finance, Arts/Humanities for creative and social sciences, IT & Vocational for technical skills, and Defense for a career in armed forces.
    2. Can I change my stream after 11th?
    Changing streams after 11th is usually difficult because the subjects are very specific. It’s better to choose carefully after 10th.
    3. Are vocational courses useful?
    Yes, vocational courses provide practical skills and can lead to jobs, entrepreneurship, or further specialized studies.
    4. How to decide the best career path?
    Identify your interests, strengths, and career goals. Seek guidance from teachers, career counselors, and online resources.
